## Building Your Team

### Starting Out

**Phase 1: Your First Knight (Levels 1-10)**
- Create one Knight (recommend Fighter for beginners)
- Focus on learning the game mechanics
- Enter open tournaments to gain experience
- Reach Level 10 to unlock core abilities

**Phase 2: Expand to Two Classes (Levels 10-20)**
- Create a Knight from a different class
- Learn the differences between classes
- Enter class-specific tournaments
- Build both Knights to Level 20

**Phase 3: Complete Your Team (Levels 20+)**
- Create your third Knight from the remaining class
- Now you have all three classes
- Enter diverse tournament types
- Specialize each Knight for their class strengths

## Team Management Strategies

### Level Distribution

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Balanced Levels" icon="scale">
    Keep all Knights at similar levels. This ensures you can enter the same tier tournaments with any Knight.
    
    **Best for**: General play, flexibility
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Specialized Levels" icon="target">
    Focus on leveling one Knight to high levels first, then level others. The high-level Knight can enter elite tournaments.
    
    **Best for**: Competitive players, maximizing one Knight
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Class Specialization" icon="users">
    Level Knights of one class higher than others. Dominate class-specific tournaments.
    
    **Best for**: Players who prefer specific strategies
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Optimizing Your Team

### Gear Distribution

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Class-Appropriate Gear" icon="helmet">
    Equip each Knight with gear that enhances their class abilities:
    - **Fighters**: Technical analysis tools
    - **Rangers**: Sentiment analysis equipment
    - **Mages**: On-chain data processors
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Best Gear to Best Knights" icon="star">
    Prioritize your strongest Knights when distributing rare gear. Maximize your competitive advantage.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Specialized Loadouts" icon="suitcase">
    Create different gear loadouts for different tournament types. Switch gear based on competition requirements.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Resource Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="$FORGE Allocation" icon="coins">
    Balance spending $FORGE across your team:
    - Don't overspend on one Knight
    - Invest in gear that benefits multiple Knights
    - Save for high-value opportunities
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="XP Focus" icon="level-up">
    Decide which Knights to prioritize for leveling:
    - Focus on your strongest Knight for elite tournaments
    - Level others to unlock tournament access
    - Balance based on your goals
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Mistakes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Neglecting Diversity" icon="exclamation-triangle">
    Don't create all Knights from one class. You'll miss opportunities in class-specific tournaments.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Overextending" icon="users">
    Don't create too many Knights too quickly. Focus on quality and learning before expanding.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ignoring Weak Knights" icon="user-slash">
    Don't abandon underperforming Knights. They may excel in different conditions or tournaments.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Poor Resource Allocation" icon="coins">
    Don't spend all resources on one Knight. Balance investments across your team.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
