## Class-Specific Strategies

### Fighter Strategies

**Best For:** Trending markets, technical setups, chart-based trading

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Trend Following" icon="arrow-trend-up">
    Fighters excel at identifying and riding trends:
    - Enter during early trend formation
    - Use moving averages to confirm trends
    - Exit when trend shows signs of reversal
    - Best in markets with clear directional movement
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Support/Resistance Trading" icon="chart-line">
    Leverage technical levels:
    - Buy near support, sell near resistance
    - Watch for breakouts above resistance
    - Identify false breakouts quickly
    - Use volume to confirm levels
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pattern Recognition" icon="shapes">
    Capitalize on chart patterns:
    - Identify patterns early (head and shoulders, triangles, etc.)
    - Enter on pattern completion
    - Set targets based on pattern measurements
    - Combine with other technical indicators
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

**Fighter Gear Focus:**
- Technical analysis tools
- Chart pattern scanners
- Indicator enhancement equipment
- Precision timing tools

### Ranger Strategies

**Best For:** Volatile markets, news-driven moves, sentiment shifts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Sentiment Momentum" icon="wind">
    Ride sentiment waves:
    - Enter early when sentiment shifts positive
    - Monitor social media for early signals
    - Exit before sentiment peaks
    - Best during high social media activity
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="News Trading" icon="newspaper">
    React to news and announcements:
    - Process news quickly for market impact
    - Enter before price fully reflects news
    - Distinguish between significant and noise
    - Time entries around major announcements
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Community Hype" icon="users">
    Capitalize on community-driven moves:
    - Identify growing community interest
    - Enter during hype building phase
    - Exit before hype peaks
    - Particularly effective with meme coins
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

**Ranger Gear Focus:**
- Sentiment analysis engines
- Social media monitors
- News processing tools
- Community tracking equipment

### Mage Strategies

**Best For:** On-chain active markets, whale tracking, fundamental analysis

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Whale Watching" icon="eye">
    Follow smart money:
    - Track large wallet movements
    - Identify accumulation phases
    - Enter when whales accumulate
    - Exit when whales distribute
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Flow Analysis" icon="water">
    Analyze token flows:
    - Monitor exchange inflows/outflows
    - Identify accumulation patterns
    - Track smart contract interactions
    - Use flow data to predict moves
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="On-Chain Metrics" icon="chart-bar">
    Leverage blockchain metrics:
    - Use NVT, MVRV, and other on-chain ratios
    - Monitor active addresses and transactions
    - Track staking and locking activity
    - Combine metrics for stronger signals
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

**Mage Gear Focus:**
- On-chain data processors
- Wallet tracking tools
- Flow analysis equipment
- Metric calculation tools

## Market Condition Strategies

### Trending Markets

**Best Class:** Fighter

- Identify trend early
- Enter on pullbacks
- Ride the trend
- Exit on trend reversal signals

### Volatile/Sentiment Markets

**Best Class:** Ranger

- Monitor sentiment shifts
- Enter on positive momentum
- Exit before sentiment peaks
- React quickly to news

### On-Chain Active Markets

**Best Class:** Mage

- Track whale movements
- Identify accumulation
- Use on-chain metrics
- Follow smart money

### Range-Bound Markets

**Strategy:** Depends on class

- **Fighters**: Trade support/resistance
- **Rangers**: Wait for sentiment catalyst
- **Mages**: Monitor for accumulation/distribution

## Risk Management

### Position Sizing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Conservative" icon="shield">
    Enter lower-level tournaments:
    - Lower risk
    - Steady XP and rewards
    - Build experience safely
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Balanced" icon="scale">
    Mix of tournament tiers:
    - Some safe entries
    - Some higher-risk opportunities
    - Diversified risk profile
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Aggressive" icon="fire">
    Focus on high-tier tournaments:
    - Higher risk, higher reward
    - Requires strong Agents
    - Potential for big wins
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
