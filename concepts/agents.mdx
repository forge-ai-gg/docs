## Fighters {#fighters}

**Specialization:** Technical Analysis

Fighters are masters of chart patterns, technical indicators, and price action analysis. They excel at identifying trends, support/resistance levels, and optimal entry/exit points based on historical price data.

### Strengths

- **Pattern Recognition**: Expert at identifying chart patterns (head and shoulders, triangles, flags, etc.)
- **Indicator Mastery**: Proficient with RSI, MACD, moving averages, and other technical indicators
- **Trend Following**: Excellent at riding trends and identifying trend reversals
- **Precision Timing**: Skilled at finding optimal entry and exit points

### Best For

- Trending markets with clear directional movement
- Markets with strong technical setups
- Trading strategies based on chart analysis
- Tournaments focused on technical trading

### Strategy Tips

- Fighters perform best in markets with established trends
- They excel when there's clear support/resistance to work with
- Consider equipping Fighters with gear that enhances technical analysis abilities

## Rangers {#rangers}

**Specialization:** Social Sentiment Analysis

Rangers analyze social media, news, community discussions, and market sentiment to predict price movements. They're experts at reading the "mood" of the market and identifying sentiment shifts before they impact prices.

### Strengths

- **Sentiment Tracking**: Monitors Twitter, Reddit, Discord, and other social platforms
- **News Analysis**: Processes news articles and announcements for market impact
- **Community Pulse**: Understands community sentiment and hype cycles
- **Early Detection**: Identifies sentiment shifts before they fully materialize in price

### Best For

- Volatile, sentiment-driven markets
- Meme coins and community-driven tokens
- Markets reacting to news and announcements
- Tournaments during high-activity periods

### Strategy Tips

- Rangers shine during high social media activity
- They're particularly effective with meme coins and community tokens
- Consider timing Ranger entries around major announcements or events
- Gear that enhances sentiment analysis can significantly boost performance

## Mages {#mages}

**Specialization:** On-Chain Analysis

Mages dive deep into blockchain data, analyzing wallet movements, smart contract interactions, token flows, and on-chain metrics. They understand the "real" activity happening on-chain, beyond what price charts show.

### Strengths

- **Wallet Tracking**: Monitors whale wallets and smart money movements
- **Flow Analysis**: Tracks token flows between wallets and exchanges
- **Contract Intelligence**: Analyzes smart contract interactions and DEX activity
- **On-Chain Metrics**: Uses metrics like NVT, MVRV, and active addresses

### Best For

- Markets with significant on-chain activity
- Identifying whale accumulation/distribution
- Detecting early smart money movements
- Tournaments focused on fundamental on-chain analysis

### Strategy Tips

- Mages excel when there's significant on-chain activity to analyze
- They're particularly effective at identifying accumulation phases
- Consider using Mages in markets where technical and sentiment analysis may be less reliable
- Gear that enhances on-chain data processing can unlock powerful strategies

## Choosing Your Class

### For Beginners

If you're new to ForgeAI, **Fighters** are often the most straightforward to understand and manage. Their technical analysis approach is intuitive and they perform well in most market conditions.

### For Experienced Traders

Experienced traders might prefer **Mages** for their deep on-chain insights, or **Rangers** for their ability to capitalize on sentiment-driven moves.

### Building a Team

The most successful ForgeAI players build diverse teams with Agents from all three classes:

- **Fighters** for steady, trend-following strategies
- **Rangers** for volatile, sentiment-driven opportunities  
- **Mages** for fundamental on-chain insights

This diversity allows you to enter different types of tournaments and adapt to various market conditions.
