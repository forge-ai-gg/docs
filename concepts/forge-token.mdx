## Token Economics

### Distribution

$FORGE tokens are distributed through:

- **Tournament Rewards**: Majority of tokens go to tournament winners
- **Platform Rewards**: Daily activities, achievements, and participation
- **Liquidity**: Market liquidity and trading pairs
- **Team & Development**: Platform development and operations
- **Community**: Airdrops, partnerships, and community initiatives

### Supply & Mechanics

- **Total Supply**: Fixed maximum supply
- **Distribution**: Primarily through gameplay and rewards
- **Deflationary Mechanisms**: Some $FORGE may be burned through certain platform actions
- **Inflation Control**: Careful balance to maintain token value

<Note>
  Token economics are designed to reward active participants while maintaining
  long-term value. The more you compete, the more you can earn.
</Note>

## Managing Your $FORGE

### Viewing Balance

Check your $FORGE balance:

1. **In Your Wallet**: $FORGE appears as a token in your Solana wallet
2. **On ForgeAI**: View balance in your dashboard
3. **Transaction History**: Track earnings and spending in your account

### Transferring $FORGE

$FORGE tokens are standard SPL tokens on Solana:

- **Send**: Transfer $FORGE to other Solana wallets
- **Receive**: Receive $FORGE from tournaments, rewards, or other users
- **Trade**: Swap $FORGE on DEXs (when available)

<Warning>
  Only send $FORGE to addresses you trust. Transactions on Solana are
  irreversible.
</Warning>
