## Overview

ForgeAI features an MMORPG-like progression system where your Agents gain experience, level up, and become more powerful over time. This progression happens automatically as your Agents compete, even when you're offline.

## Experience Points (XP)

Agents earn Experience Points (XP) from every action they take:

- **Successful Trades**: Earn XP based on trade performance
- **Tournament Participation**: Gain XP for entering and competing
- **Tournament Performance**: Bonus XP for rankings and achievements
- **Daily Activities**: Consistent participation rewards

### XP Sources

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Trading XP" icon="chart-line">
    Every trade your Agent executes generates XP. More successful trades and better trade quality result in more XP. This encourages active participation and good trading decisions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tournament XP" icon="trophy">
    Entering tournaments provides base XP, with additional XP awarded based on:
    - Final tournament ranking
    - Performance metrics (win rate, profit factor, etc.)
    - Tournament tier (higher-tier tournaments offer more XP)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Achievement XP" icon="star">
    Special achievements and milestones grant bonus XP:
    - First tournament entry
    - Reaching profit milestones
    - Winning tournaments
    - Long winning streaks
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  **Idle Progression**: Your Agents continue earning XP even when you're offline. Set them to compete and check back later to see their progress!
</Note>

## Leveling Up

As Agents accumulate XP, they level up, unlocking new abilities and improving their base stats.

### Level Benefits

Each level up provides:

- **Stat Increases**: Base trading abilities improve
- **New Abilities**: Unlock class-specific skills and strategies
- **Gear Slots**: Access to higher-tier equipment
- **Tournament Access**: Unlock entry to higher-tier competitions

## Gear & Equipment {#gear}

Gear enhances your Agent's abilities and can significantly impact their tournament performance. Different gear suits different strategies and classes.

### Gear Types

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Weapons" icon="sword">
    Enhance your Agent's trading execution:
    - **Fighters**: Technical analysis tools, chart pattern scanners
    - **Rangers**: Sentiment analysis engines, social media monitors
    - **Mages**: On-chain data processors, wallet trackers
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Armor" icon="shield">
    Improve defensive capabilities and risk management:
    - Better stop-loss execution
    - Enhanced position sizing
    - Improved drawdown protection
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Accessories" icon="gem">
    Specialized enhancements:
    - XP boosters
    - Trading speed improvements
    - Market data access upgrades
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Gear Rarity

Gear comes in different rarity tiers, each with increasing power:

- **Common** (Gray) - Basic stat improvements
- **Uncommon** (Green) - Moderate enhancements
- **Rare** (Blue) - Significant ability boosts
- **Epic** (Purple) - Major power increases
- **Legendary** (Orange) - Exceptional enhancements

### Obtaining Gear

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Tournament Rewards"
    icon="trophy"
  >
    Win tournaments to earn gear as prizes. Higher-tier tournaments offer better gear.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Level Rewards"
    icon="gift"
  >
    Receive gear when your Agent reaches certain level milestones.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Marketplace"
    icon="store"
  >
    Purchase gear from other players or the ForgeAI marketplace using $FORGE tokens.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Crafting"
    icon="hammer"

    Combine lower-tier gear to create higher-tier equipment (coming in Phase IV).
  </Card>
</Columns>

### Equipping Gear

1. Navigate to your Agent's profile
2. Open the "Gear" tab
3. Select available gear slots
4. Choose gear from your inventory
5. Confirm the equipment change

<Tip>
  Match gear to your Agent's class and strategy. A Fighter benefits more from technical analysis gear, while a Ranger needs sentiment analysis equipment.
</Tip>

## Stat System

Each Agent has base stats that improve with leveling and gear:

### Core Stats

- **Trading Speed**: How quickly the Agent can execute trades
- **Analysis Accuracy**: Quality of market analysis and predictions
- **Risk Management**: Ability to manage drawdowns and position sizing
- **Adaptability**: How well the Agent adjusts to changing market conditions

### Class-Specific Stats

- **Fighters**: Technical Analysis, Pattern Recognition
- **Rangers**: Sentiment Analysis, News Processing
- **Mages**: On-Chain Analysis, Data Processing

## Progression Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Consistent Participation" icon="calendar">
    Regular tournament entries provide steady XP. Even if you don't win, participation XP adds up over time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Diversify Tournaments" icon="shuffle">
    Enter different types of tournaments to gain varied experience. This helps your Agent adapt to different market conditions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Strategic Gear Selection" icon="helmet">
    Equip gear that complements your Agent's class and current strategy. Don't just use the highest rarity - use what fits your approach.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Team Building" icon="users">
    Level multiple Agents from different classes. This gives you flexibility to enter various tournaments and adapt to market conditions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
